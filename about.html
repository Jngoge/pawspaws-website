About Pawspaws

Built by dog lovers,
for dog lovers.

We believe every dog deserves an owner who truly understands them — and we're on a mission to make that possible.

Our story

Why we started Pawspaws

Pawspaws was born from a simple frustration: too many dog owners are given contradictory, outdated, or fear-based advice that damages the bond between them and their dogs.

We set out to build a resource grounded in modern behavioural science — positive, kind, and actually effective. The result is a growing library of training and wellness guidance, and a book that has already helped thousands of dogs and owners thrive.

Our approach is simple: understand your dog, communicate clearly, and reward what you want to see more of. It works for puppies. It works for rescues. It works for every breed.

Our values

What we stand for

🔬

Science-backed

Every technique we teach is grounded in animal behaviour science — not tradition, not myth.

💚

Force-free

We never recommend punishment, dominance theory, or aversive tools. Ever.

🤝

Relationship-first

Training is about communication and trust — not control. A better relationship leads to better behaviour.

🌍

Accessible to all

Great dog ownership shouldn't require expensive private trainers. We make expert guidance available to everyone.
